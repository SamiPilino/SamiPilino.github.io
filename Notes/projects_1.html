My Development Process

This project is an attempt at codifying my development process. It will most likely change over time, and the methods and tools I use now probably won't be the same methods and tools I use later on in my career. I find that explicitly outlining such processes can help ensure that important tasks don't get accidentally skipped or end up getting executed in the wrong order.
