About

Greetings! My name is Samuel D. Villegas. I'm a recent Oregon State University Computer Science graduate (Go Beavs!) open to opportunities to work on all sorts of interesting projects.

My interests include, but are not limited to:

  • Systems that are scalable and/or incrementally deployable
  • Knowledge systems that leverage data (big and small) to help people make more informed decisions
  • Embedded systems
  • GNU/Linux administration
  • Operating systems
  • Distributed systems
  • Enterprise resource planning (ERP)
  • Business Intelligence / Decision Intelligence
  • Mathematical music composition
  • Programming tools that help other people do their jobs more effectively
  • Project management
  • Domain-specific languages (DSL's)
  • Articulatory phonetics

And I also have a goal of one day programming a space robot or some other system out in space.

Unfortunately, I don't have the time to pursue every single interest of mine to the depth I'd like to dive to. But, fortunately, that's just another problem to solve! I'm in the process of developing a system that helps organize, prioritize, and schedule my interests for me so I can focus more on the actual projects themselves. I'm not super sure how that'll go—maybe I'll just end up re-inventing the wheel—but I'm excited for where it may take me and what additional insights and perspectives I'll gain along the way.

November 15, 2021
